Welcome to the movie ranking!

The top 3 movies are :

1. Now you see me (2013) :

This is one of my favourite magician movie of all time!
This movie revolves around a revenge which a guy has
to take on a rich businessman, where he uses 4 great
magicians to make his revenge successful.

2. Doctor strange (2016) :

Being a huge fan of Marvel comics, this is one of the best
among them all. A doctor, who has met with an accident,
seeks help from a place called Kamar taj, where he faces
many vulnerabilities in multiverse. This movie has a mind
bending vfx what will keep the audience in awe.

3. Queen :

This is a movie about a heartbroken woman who's fianace
cancels the wedding. She goes to a solo trip to New york
and Paris, which made her realise that there is much more
in the world to explore, with friends rather than being
heartbroken.
